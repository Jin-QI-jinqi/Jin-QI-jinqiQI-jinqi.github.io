Academic Background

I serve as the Center for Aging Science Associate Director, the UG Programs Coordinator, and an Associate Professor in the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics. Before my role at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, I was a visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Management Sciences. I earned my PhD from the NUS Business School at the National University of Singapore.

Research Information

My research interests include healthcare operations, robust optimization, transportation, and target-based decision-making. I aim to advance the development of theories in these relevant fields and provide support for practical applications. Through using data modeling, data analytics, and other methods, I strive to improve the scientificity and efficiency of decisions and help solve complicated questions in the real world.
